Post traumatic stress disorder is a mental health condition that can occur after experiencing or witnessing a traumatic event. It can cause distressing thoughts, emotions, and nightmares related to the traumatic event. People with PTSD may avoid reminders of the event, be easily startled, and have trouble sleeping. Treatment usually involves therapy and sometimes medication to help cope with symptoms.

Anxiety is a mental health condition that causes excessive worry and fear. It can make you feel restless, irritable, and have trouble concentrating. Treatment can involve therapy and medication to help reduce symptoms and improve well-being.

SMART Recovery

Addiction Support Group

Addiction is a condition where a person becomes dependent on a substance or behavior, often leading to harmful consequences. It can involve substances like drugs or alcohol, or behaviors like gambling or excessive gaming. Addiction causes cravings and a loss of control, making it difficult to stop. Treatment for addiction typically involves therapy, support groups, and sometimes medication.
